Faculty members from the School of Computer Science Engineering and Information Systems possess expertise in various cutting-edge technology domains. The school actively engages in research and development (R&D) projects sponsored by both international and national funding agencies. Faculty members have successfully secured funds from prestigious organizations such as the European Union and the British Council. National funding agencies, including the Department of Science and Technology (DST), the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR), and the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO), are currently supporting several ongoing projects within the school.Additionally, faculty members benefit from the VIT seed grant, which provides initial funding for project startups.
